# Announcement Guidelines

These guidelines cover how accelerator teams should communicate their participation in the OrbitFlare Infra Accelerator and their mainnet launch.

---

## When You Can Announce

- **Acceptance**: You may announce your acceptance into the program after your application PR has been merged.
- **Launch**: You should publish a mainnet launch announcement once your product is live and stable.

---

## Infrastructure Attribution

All accelerator teams are expected to include "Powered by OrbitFlare" infrastructure attribution in at least one of the following locations:

- Project documentation or GitHub README
- Launch announcements or public threads
- Website infrastructure credits
- Technical architecture documentation

OrbitFlare will provide a "Powered by OrbitFlare Infrastructure" badge that teams may include in their documentation, repositories, or product pages.

---

## Acceptance Announcements

When announcing your acceptance, you may:

- State that you have been accepted into the OrbitFlare Infra Accelerator
- Describe what the program provides (infrastructure support, engineering access, etc.)
- Share your excitement and goals for the program

When announcing your acceptance, do not:

- Describe the relationship as a "partnership", "collaboration", or "strategic alliance"
- Imply that OrbitFlare has invested in or endorsed your project specifically
- Make claims about being the "first" or "only" project to build something on Solana
- Share details about the internal program structure or other cohort teams without their consent

---

## Mainnet Launch Announcements

Each team should publish a mainnet launch thread or announcement covering:

- **What you built** - Describe the product and what it does
- **The journey** - Share highlights from the path from prototype to mainnet
- **Challenges solved** - What technical problems did you overcome?
- **Infrastructure** - Acknowledge the infrastructure supporting your deployment

Examples of appropriate attribution:

- "Built on Solana. Powered by OrbitFlare infrastructure."
- "Our mainnet deployment runs on OrbitFlare RPC for reliable transaction delivery."
- "Thanks to OrbitFlare for the infrastructure support and engineering guidance during our path to mainnet."

---

## Social Media

- Tag @OrbitFlareNodes in launch-related posts so we can amplify
- OrbitFlare may reshare or quote your announcements
- OrbitFlare may publish a Cohort Launch Announcement featuring all teams that successfully completed the program

---

## Review Process

We do not require pre-approval of announcements. However, if you would like OrbitFlare to review a draft before publishing, reach out through your private channel and we will provide feedback within 1-2 business days.
